How to make Chicken Parmesan

Chicken Parmesan is no doubt the most popular non-pasta Italian-American Dish. And while this delicious meal may seem fancy, it's actually pretty easy to make at home. In fact, chicken Parmesan is one of those meals that's often best made in your own kitchen.

The best Chick Parmesan recipe, made healthy.

Ingredients

Recipe creates 8 servings

  • 8 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 3/4 cup seasoned breadcrumbs, preferably whole wheat
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter, melted
  • 3/4 reduced fat mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • Cooking spray of choice

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C)
  2. Spray a large baking sheet lightly with cooking spray
  3. Combine breadcrumbs and parmesan cheese in a bowl
  4. Melt butter in another bowl and light brush over each chicken breast
  5. Dip the chicken into breadcrumb mixture and then place on baking sheet
  6. Lightly spray the prepped chicken with cooking spray
  7. Bake for 25 minutes
  8. Remove from oven and top each chicken with 1 tbsp of marinara sauce, and 1 1/2 tbsp of mozzarella cheese
  9. Bake for another 5 min or until cheese is melted. Enteral temperature should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C)
  10. Enjoy!
