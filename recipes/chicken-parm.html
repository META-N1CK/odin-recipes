Chicken Parmesan

How to make Chicken Parmesan

Chicken Parmesan is no doubt the most popular non-pasta Italian-American Dish. And while this delicious meal may seem fancy, it's actually pretty easy to make at home. In fact, chicken Parmesan is one of those meals that's often best made in your own kitchen.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 471 calories; fat 24.9g; carbohydrates 24.8g; protein 42.1g

Ingredients

Recipe creates 4 servings

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup bread crumbs, or more as needed
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese, divided
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour, more if needed
  • 1/2 cup olive oil for frying, or as needed
  • 1/2 cup prepared tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup fresh mozzarella, cut into small cubes
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/2 cup grated provolone cheese
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C)
  2. Firmly pound chicken with the smooth side of a meat mallet to a thickness of 1/2-inch
  3. Season chicken thoroughly with salt and pepper
  4. Beat eggs in a bowl and set aside
  5. Mix bread crumbs and 1/2 cup parmesan cheese in a separate bowl, set aside
  6. Sift flour over chicken breast, evenly coat both sides
  7. Dip the flour-coated chicken breast in the beaten eggs and then transfer to the bread crumb mixture.
  8. Pressure the bread crumbs into both sides and let the chicken rest for 10 to 15 minutes
  9. Heat 1/2-inch olive oil in a large skillet on medium-high heat until it begins to shimmer
  10. Cook chicken in the hot oil until golden, about 2 minutes per side
  11. Transfer chicken to a baking pan and top each breast with 2 tablespoons of tomato sauce
  12. Layer each chicken breast with equal amounts of mozzarella cheese, fresh basil, and provolone cheese
  13. Sprinkle remaining parmesan over top and drizzle each with 1/2 teaspoon olive oil
  14. Bake in the oven until cheese is browned and bubbly, 15 to 20 minutes. Enteral temperature should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C)
  15. Enjoy!
