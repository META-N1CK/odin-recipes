Worlds Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 298 Calories; protein 3.6g; carbohydrates 38.9g; fat 15.6g

Ingredients

Recipe creates 24 servings

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 tablespoon and 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 tablespoon and 1/2 teaspoon hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts
