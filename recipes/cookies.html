Protein Chocolate Chip Cookies

Cookies? Healthy?

Whats better than cookies? Chocolate cookies packed with protein that is also low in calories. Perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth without killing your diet goals. Enjoy with your nut milk of choice.

Ingredients

Recipe creates 10 servings

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup PB2 peanut powder
  • 1/4 cup sweetener of choice
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened applesauce
  • 50g chocolate protein powder
  • 2 tbsp self-rising flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp mini sugar-free chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. In a bowl, whisk in the large egg, peanut powder, sweetener, and applesauce. This is the wet bowl
  3. In another bowl, mix in the chocolate protein powder, self-rising flour, cocoa powder, and baking soda. This is the dry bowl
  4. While mixing the wet bowl, gradually add the dry bowl ingredients into the wet bowl. Mix thoroughly until smooth
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips
  6. Grease a baking pan
  7. Split cookie dough into 10 balls
  8. Bake for 6-8 minutes. (Cooking time may vary depending on protein used)
  9. Let cookie cool slightly on a cooling rack
  10. Enjoy!
