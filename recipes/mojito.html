What is a Mojito?

A mojito is a class Cuban cocktail the is made from a few simple ingredients. This drink is the perfect balance of sweet and tart, not to mention refreshing! The recipe makes one serving, but you can easily scale it up to make a pitcher for summertime entertaining.

Ingredients

Recipe creates 1 serving

  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2 medium lime, cut into 3 wedges, divided
  • 1 tbsp sweetener of choice, or to taste
  • 1 cup ice cubes, or as needed
  • 1 fl oz white rum
  • 1/2 cup lime flavored club soda, or as needed

Directions

  1. Place lime wedges into glass
  2. Smack 8 mint leaves in hand and then place into the glass
  3. Add your sweetener of choice to the glass
  4. Muddle the ingredients in the glass
  5. Fill glass almost to the top with ice
  6. Add in the rum and fill with the lime club soda
  7. Stir, taste, add more sweetener if desired
  8. Enjoy!
